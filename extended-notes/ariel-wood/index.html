Craft /kraft/: noun

A manifestation of physical/technical knowledge. It can mean a level of care toward a material or object, and it can also mean a display of ego, but sometimes a balance can be established—one can utilize craft when it's also the conceptual frame for the piece.

A Collection by Ariel Wood

Craft is a form of alchemy.

Rachael Starbuck
